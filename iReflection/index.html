The Today canvas

Start each day with a blank page.

No prompts. No templates. Just you, a clean page, and the day's date at the top. Pick a blank, ruled, or dotted background — whatever helps you write.

Apple Pencil, rendered crisply

Your words, your pace.

Every stroke is saved automatically with PencilKit, so what you write today still looks right when you zoom in months from now. Switch between portrait and landscape when the mood changes.

Calendar & streak

See your history at a glance.

A month view lights up every day you've written and counts the streak you've built. Tap any filled day to re-read exactly what you wrote — the way you wrote it.

Privacy

Private by design.
Not by policy.

iReflection does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you write stays on your iPad, in the app's sandboxed container. No account. No cloud. No analytics. No network requests. Ever.
