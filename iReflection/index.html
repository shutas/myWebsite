iReflection

A private daily handwriting journal for your iPad. Pick up your Apple Pencil and write — that's it.

iPadOS 18+ No account No cloud No analytics

Just you, a clean page, and the day's date

iReflection turns your iPad into a private handwritten journal. There are no prompts, no templates, and no social features — the same way you would write on paper, but with the clarity and permanence of a dedicated digital canvas.

Every entry is saved automatically and organized by date. A calendar view shows your writing history at a glance, with filled-in days and a running streak that gently encourages you to keep the habit going.

Features

Write with Apple Pencil

A clean PencilKit canvas rendered crisply at any zoom level. Portrait or landscape, blank, ruled, or dotted.

Daily streak

A calendar shows every day you've written and a running streak you can protect.

On-device only

Entries live in the app's sandbox on your iPad. No server, no sync, no account.

Daily reminders

Optional local notification if you want a nudge at a time you choose.

Light & Dark Mode

Semantic iPadOS colors that adapt automatically. Beautiful either way.

Accessible

Full VoiceOver support and Dynamic Type throughout the interface.

Privacy, built in

Short version: iReflection does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you write stays on your iPad.

No accounts. No sign-in. No analytics SDKs. No advertising identifiers. No network requests. Read the full privacy policy.
